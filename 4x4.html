How to Play:

R - Rotate Right face
L - Rotate Left face
U - Rotate Up face
D - Rotate Down face
F - Rotate Front face
B - Rotate Back face
r - Inner Right
l - Inner Left
u - Inner Up
d - Inner Down
f - Inner Front
b - Inner Back
1 - Normal direction
2 - Reverse direction
T - Toggle reverse
Ctrl+Z - Undo move
Drag - Rotate cube
Scroll - Zoom in/out
